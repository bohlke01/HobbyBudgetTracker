        üìò Getting Started

        üéØ Welcome to Hobby Budget Tracker!

        This application helps you track expenses and time spent on your hobbies. Here's how to get started:

        1. Add a Hobby: Go to the Hobbies tab and create your first hobby (e.g., Photography, Gaming, Cooking)
        2. Track Expenses: Record what you spend on equipment, materials, or services for your hobby
        3. Log Activities: Track how much time you spend enjoying your hobby
        4. View Statistics: Check your Summary to see the cost per hour for each hobby
        üí° Quick Tip: The key metric is "Cost per Hour" - this shows how much you're spending per hour of enjoyment. Lower values mean better value for your time!

        Cost per Hour = Total Expenses √∑ Total Hours

        üìä How do I see detailed statistics for a hobby?
        Click on any hobby in the Summary tab or Hobbies tab to open a detailed view with statistics and a chart showing how your cost per hour changes over time.
        ‚è±Ô∏è Can I enter activities in minutes?
        Yes! When adding an activity, you can use both the Hours and Minutes fields. For example, enter 1 hour and 30 minutes for a 1.5-hour activity. The app will automatically convert it to decimal hours.

        Go to the Import/Export tab and click "Export Data". This will download a JSON file with all your hobbies, expenses, and activities. Keep this file safe as a backup. You can import it later to restore your data.

        üí° Tips & Best Practices

        üìù Tracking Tips

        • Be Consistent: Log your activities regularly to get accurate cost per hour metrics
        • Include All Expenses: Don't forget small purchases - they add up!
        • Use Descriptions: Add notes to expenses and activities to remember what they were for
        • Set Realistic Targets: Start with a comfortable target cost per hour and adjust as needed

        üìä Understanding Your Data

        • High Cost per Hour? You might have expensive equipment costs. This can improve as you spend more time with your hobby
        • Low Cost per Hour? Great! You're getting good value from your hobby spending
        • Track Over Time: Initial costs are often higher (equipment purchases). The chart shows how this improves
        üí° Pro Tip: Hobbies with high initial equipment costs (like photography or gaming) will have high cost per hour at first. This naturally decreases as you spend more time enjoying your hobby!
